ABOUT TEDxIITBHU

TED is a non-profit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. It started out in 1984 as a conference bringing together people from three worlds: Technology, Entertainment, and Design. This event is under an official license under TED headquartered at New York.
Since its inception 4 years ago, TEDxIITBHU has grown exponentially, becoming one of the largest university TEDx events in the country! We have not just inspired students of our institute, but also made an impact globally garnering over 7,00,000 views on our TEDx videos on YouTube! With an aim to broadcast ideas worth spreading, TEDxIITBHU has hosted stalwart speakers from all walks of life over the past 4 years, who time and again dazzled the audience with their diverse perspectives and incredible experiences.




Welcome to a perfect theme

"Shades"


This edition's theme is called 'Shades'. As the world progresses headlong into the future, change seems to be the only constant. Perhaps it is in our nature to appreciate that change. Dimensions, Perceptions, Verticals: different names to the same shade. The piece is about how the same is viewed differently by people, over time, over space. It is in celebration of this role of contrast that TEDxIITBHU invites you on a journey across the globe, across time, and across lives.
